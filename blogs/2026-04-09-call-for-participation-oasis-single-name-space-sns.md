A new OASIS Technical Committee is being formed. The OASIS Single Name Space (SNS) TC has been proposed by the members of OASIS listed in the charter below. The TC name, statement of purpose, scope, list of deliverables, audience, IPR mode, and language specified in this proposal will constitute the TC’s official charter.
