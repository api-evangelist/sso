By Paul Wormeli, Chair, NIEMOpen Project Governing Board OASIS Open has formally submitted two NIEMOpen standards to ISO/IEC Joint Technical Committee 1 (JTC 1) for transposition into International Standards. The two submissions are NIEM Model v6.0 and the Naming and Design Rules (NDR) v6.0, both approved as OASIS Standards in December 2025. This is a significant moment.
